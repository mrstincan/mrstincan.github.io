Despite the others' confirmation, he doubted the truth of it. He hoped it was true, oh Celestia how he hoped, but there was something inside him... a feeling, strong enough to creep in his entire body and break through the stone cold façade of the former Geo Archon. ─ Fear. Was this all a dream? Did he fall asleep and dream of Guizhong coming back? Is he in a dream, chasing what was lost all those millennia ago? Was it real? What would he do if it was? What would he do if it wasn't? It had to be real. It just had to be real. The closer he got to the forest, the louder his heart rang in his ears, and here he thought it lost its ability to beat. Zhongli was not afraid of the beasts he'd find in the forest, but rather what he wouldn't find. A silent prayer to all he knew holy caused but the slightest crack, letting warm tears run down his stone cold expression. Suddenly, he held his breath. Through his tears, the blurry image of the snow covered forest almost made it look like a field of Glaze Lilies... and there she stood, the most beautiful Glaze Lilly out of them all. Just a moment ago he felt like crumbling to pieces but as soon as he caught a glimpse of her it was as if he solidified ─ stiff and frozen ─ like a statue. It was a miracle his lips allowed for her name to trespass. "Guizhong... it's you."
